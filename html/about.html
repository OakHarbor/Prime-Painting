About Prime Painting

Thank you for stopping by our page! Prime Painting is proudly ran by Las Vegas Natives, Frankie and Andria Baca, along with their Grandfather Dennis Gana.

Frankie had always dreamed of owning and running his own business and in 2018, he made the scary decision to take the challenge, leaving his 7 year long “secure” job as a production supervisor in the industrial field. Frankie has always been the type to strive for perfection and works hard each and every day to ensure the company’s growth and success.

Dennis, Frankie’s Grandfather, taught him everything he knows in the painting industry. Dennis, whose Father was also a painter, has over 50 years of experience in the painting field as well as over 20 years of experience owning his own successful businesses. Dennis has an eye for detail which he has passed onto Frankie and both are sure to instill these standards to their amazing team.

Andria, Frankie’s wife is the brains of the outfit, handling all of fine details in the background in order to keep the wheels turning!

Together, we strive to be the absolute best painting contractor in Southern NV, providing all of our clients and potential clients with a great experience from the very first call to the last touch up! We all work extremely hard to ensure great communication, fair prices, quality work, cleanliness, and respect for everyone!

Thank you again for your interest in Prime Painting and we truly look forward to working with you!

Frankie Baca

Owner of Prime Painting
